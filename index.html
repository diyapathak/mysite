Diya Pathak

Bio: Diya Pathak is a high school student at Cupertino High school in California. Along with being a magazine and blog writer at Her Culture, she leads the Women's Empowerment Association at her school. She enjoys giving back to her community, whether it is tutoring neighbor children, or giving family tours and inspiring kids about technology at the local museum. In her free time, Diya loves to sing, dance, listen to music, and spend time with her family and friends.
